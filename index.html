About Me

I'm Indra, a brand designer specializing in logos and visual identities from Indonesia. For over 5 years, I've helped brands build bold, strategic, and distinctive identities—for those who don't want to look like everyone else.

I work with purpose-driven brands, visionary startups, and individuals with progressive mindsets who want to stand out authentically, not just follow trends.

Whether you're building a brand from scratch or redefining an existing identity, I combine creative strategy, a strong visual system, and meaningful storytelling to create an identity that is relevant, memorable, and has a lasting impact.
